About the Memory Album

Welcome to your personal streaming platform dedicated exclusively to memories. This website is styled as a Netflix-like environment, designed to celebrate the most valuable, cinematic, and emotional chapters of life.

Each profile represents a different timeframe or theme, letting you filter through years or special moments with smooth transitions, responsive row sliders, and a details overlay.

Album Controls:

  • Profiles: Click on the profiles list in the top right to switch between 2024, 2025, 2026, and Dusk & Dawn.
  • Search: Use the search icon in the navigation bar to filter all memories instantly by title, description, or date.
  • Favorites: Mark memories with the heart icon to add them to your personalized Favorites rail.
  • Lightbox: Expand any card to see details in full size and high definition.
